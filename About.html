About Us

The Ministry of Health in the Kingdom of Bahrain is the government body responsible for overseeing and developing the healthcare system in the country. Established to ensure the health and wellbeing of all citizens and residents, the Ministry provides comprehensive healthcare services through its network of hospitals, health centers, and specialized facilities.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, accessible healthcare services while promoting public health and preventing diseases through innovative programs and policies.

Our Vision

A healthy population enjoying the highest standards of healthcare services in a sustainable environment.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, high-quality healthcare services through integrated systems and qualified professionals.
